What the behaviour looks like

Two fronts are moving at once.

Consumer behaviour

  • Growth in ChatGPT usage
  • Growth in Perplexity
  • AI-assisted search
  • AI-generated recommendations
  • Shopping through AI assistants
  • Zero-click behaviour
  • Declining traditional search traffic

Enterprise behaviour

  • AI procurement agents
  • Customer service agents
  • Personal AI assistants

Gartner forecasts AI agents intermediating $15T of B2B purchasing by 2028 — the enterprise side is moving too.
